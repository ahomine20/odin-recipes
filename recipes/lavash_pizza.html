Lavash Pizza

Description

Lavash pizza is an exceptionally quick and easy method to prepare a thin-crust pizza, often serving as an ideal appetizer or a fast light meal. The dish utilizes large, thin sheets of lavash flatbread as a crisp, pre-made base, eliminating the long preparation time typically required for traditional pizza dough.

The lavash is simply topped with classic pizza ingredients like tomato sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni before being baked briefly until the cheese is melted and golden. The simplicity of the recipe allows for quick customization with any desired toppings, making it a versatile and fast solution for satisfying pizza cravings in minutes.

ingredients

  • 2 package lavash flatbread

  • 1 cup pizza sauce

  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded

  • 4 ounces pepperoni, or as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Spray one side of lavash with nonstick spray.

  2. Bake lavash directly on the rack in the preheated oven for 2 minutes.

  3. Carefully remove lavash from oven; place on a baking tray. Spread 2 to 3 tablespoons pizza sauce on each lavash.

  4. Sprinkle 2 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese on each lavash. Top with pepperoni.

  5. Bake pizza until cheese is melted and golden, about 15 minutes.

  6. Enjoy with someone!
